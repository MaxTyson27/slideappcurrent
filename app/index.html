Slide App

is a lifestyle Web3 App with SocialFi and GameFi elements.

  • 1

    The user needs to buy an NFT-skateboard.

  • 4

    Users follow each other and like videos.

  • 2

    The user gets an NFT-skateboard, X SGT game tokens (Slide Gaming Token) and a charged virtual battery for a video camera.

  • 5

    Likes can be exchanged for SM (Slide Movie) exchange tokens.

  • 3

    With the help of a charged battery, the user can create skateboarding videos right in the application and upload them to the news feed.

  • 6

    When a user likes other videos, their game tokens are burned and the battery is charged for new videos.

The role of SGT and SM

  • SGT:
  • 1. The ability to «like» content
  • 2. Skateboard repairs
  • 3. Battery charging
  • 4. Skateboard Upgrade
  • SM:
  • 1. Content rewards
  • 2. Skateboard Upgrade
  • 3. Exchange for SGT

Ways to get SGT and SM

  • SGT:
  • 1. Purchasing a skate
  • 2. Exchange with SM in the wallet
  • 3. Viewing content inside the app
  • 4. Remuneration for content moderation
  • SM:
  • 1. Getting likes and sharing in the wallet
  • 2. Purchases on the stock exchange

Burning SGT and SM

  • SGT:
  • 1. Likes
  • 2. Skateboard repairs
  • 3. Skateboard Upgrade
  • SM:
  • 1. Exchange for SGT
  • 2. Skateboard Upgrade

We charge small taxes on activities within the application, such as NFT-skateboard trading, repairs and upgrade of NFT-skateboards, as well as on advertising. All assets in Slide belong to individuals.
